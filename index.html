Shell Hell

Game Description


There are 3 viscous, bloodthirsty dragons that need to be defeated. The player plays as Greg the Hero in this action-packed platformer. The player will play through 3 unique levels, where they will have to dodge obstacles such as pits and spikes. The dragons will be shooting fireballs at the player while they are attempting to defeat them.

Play Now and help Greg the Hero defeat the 3 Mighty Dragons!

Game Controls


  • Move Forward: W
  • Move Back: S
  • Shoot: Left Mouse Button
  • R: Restart
